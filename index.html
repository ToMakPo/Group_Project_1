Let's Play!

GOAL

Try and trick your friends with the best wrong answers to earn the most points.

Stage-1

In Trick Trivia, players will be shown a question and then you will need to provide the best wrong answer.

Stage-2

Next, players will be given a list of answers. One of them will be the correct answer, but the rest will be a mix of the wrong answers players provided. If you've played your chance well, you will have tricked everyone else into picking your wrong answer. You will get:

  • 3 points for the correct answer.
  • 2 additional points for being the first to provide the correct answer.
  • 1 additional point for every player that picked your Trick answer.

When Trick Trivia questions are over, the one with the most points wins.
