Trick Trivia

Welcome to Trick Trivia!

Rules:

The goal of this game is to trick your friends and get the most points to win. There are two stages to this game; setup and guessing.

In the setup stage, you will be shown a question and you will be able to provide a believable answer that is NOT correct. You will only have 30 seconds to come up with the answer and submit it.

In the guessing stage, you will be given a list of answers. One of them will be the correct answer, but the rest will be a mix of the ones provided by your friends and ones provided by the game. You will have to select the correct answer. If you get the correct answer, you will get 3 points. If you are the first to select the correct answer, you will get an additional 2 points. If someone else picked the answer you gave in the setup stage, then you will get one more point for each person picking your answer.

When the game is over, the one with the most points wins.
